Breakdown of Ancestries by Percentage

is Humans make up the vast majority in the area around the Inner Sea of Galorian. This population breakdown is what I use for random character generation and NPC's:

  1. Human: 70% (01-70 on a d100)
  2. Elves: 8% (71-78)
  3. Dwarves: 7% (79-85)
  4. Halflings: 5% (86-90)
  5. Gnomes: 4% (91-94)
  6. Uncommon Races: 4% (95-98)
  7. Rare Races: 2% (99-00)
